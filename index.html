About

A bit about me

I help organisations design better ways to measure impact, generate evidence, and learn from their work. My expertise spans monitoring, evaluation and learning (MEL), AI for evaluation, outcome harvesting, learning agendas, theories of change, results frameworks, impact measurement, data systems, dashboards, and evidence synthesis. I enjoy turning complex programme data into practical insights that support better decisions.

My background in GIS and spatial analysis shaped the way I approach evaluation today. I think in systems, looking beyond individual indicators to understand the patterns and relationships that drive change. Whether I'm designing an evaluation, building a dashboard, or strengthening an organisation's evidence systems, my goal is to create approaches that are rigorous, practical, and built to be used.

Beyond consulting, I share insights on data, evaluation, and AI through @_everythingdata and am developing an original research concept called Cognitive Capture, which explores how AI-driven measurement systems can influence professional judgment, and how we can ensure technology strengthens rather than replaces human expertise.
