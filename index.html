About

A bit about me

I help organisations design better ways to measure impact, generate evidence, and learn from their work, working at the intersection of established MEL methods and newer, AI-driven evaluation tools — turning complex data into decisions people actually act on.

My background in GIS and spatial analysis shaped how I think about evaluation. I look past individual indicators to the patterns and relationships driving change, whether I'm designing an evaluation, building a dashboard, or rebuilding an organisation's evidence systems from the ground up.

I write about data, evaluation and AI through @_everythingdata.

Projects & case studies

Real engagements from my work in MEL system design, dashboards and impact analysis.

MEL DESIGN

LIF Double Down Initiative

MY ROLE

MEL lead — designed the framework end to end and owned it through rollout.

CHALLENGE

A programme launching across Kenya, Uganda and Nigeria, running two distinct tracks with staggered kickoffs, needed one coherent way to track progress instead of parallel, disconnected systems.

APPROACH

Designed the MEL framework, logframe and IPTT, including a dedicated Champions survey instrument, structured for comparability across countries and tracks despite the different timelines.

IMPACT

Gave the programme team a single, comparable view of performance across three countries and two tracks.

DASHBOARD

AFIRR/BRD HATANA Dashboard Rebuild

MY ROLE

Led the dashboard rebuild, from requirements through to stakeholder handover.

CHALLENGE

Reporting for World Bank and BRD stakeholders relied on static reports that were slow to update and hard to interrogate directly.

APPROACH

Rebuilt the dashboard from the ground up, replacing static exports with a live, interactive view of programme indicators.

IMPACT

Stakeholders could explore the data themselves instead of waiting on the next static report cycle.

ANALYSIS

FIRST+II Impact Analysis — CapPlus & Mastercard Foundation

MY ROLE

Designed and ran the analysis that underpinned the report's headline findings.

CHALLENGE

The annual impact report needed credible, defensible evidence of business growth in revenue, profit and employment that could withstand scrutiny.

APPROACH

Designed a matched-pair analysis using CAGR methodology with IQR-based outlier removal to keep the comparisons statistically sound.

IMPACT

Formed the analytical backbone of the FIRST+II Annual Impact Report delivered to CapPlus and Mastercard Foundation.
